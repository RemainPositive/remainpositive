Remain Positive answers the negative campaigning of both sides of the referendum debate with the core principles that we believe should guide our decision. Love is greater than fear. Unity better than division.

We're not affiliated with any political party, and we think Britain is better off in Europe. Don't give up hope. Vote remain on 23rd June.

People across the continent face the same challenges as us. From the economy to climate change to the Internet, we'd be kidding ourselves to act like we're not connected. Let's celebrate our interdependence, and stand tall together to face the future.

Peace

The period of peace since the Second World War has been the longest for 2000 years. It's been built on careful foundations and respect between nations. Let's stay true to that vision, even when times are tough.

The average Brit shares DNA with people all over the continent. But Europe's cultural diversity runs deep, from time-honoured traditions to expressions of love and identity that know no borders. Let's celebrate our differences with pride.
